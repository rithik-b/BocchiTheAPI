Bocchle is a game where you have to guess the episode of **Bocchi the Rock** on screen.

You get **6 tries** to guess the right episode using the keypad. Type your answer in 2 digits (**06** for episode 6 for example).

If the screenshot is from an ED, you can type any episode that ED appears in (like 01 for ED1 in episode 1).

Please enjoy!
